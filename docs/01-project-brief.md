# KEYFO Restaurant & Cafe - Project Brief

## Goal
Build a production-quality, mobile-first, SEO-friendly and AI-readable one-page restaurant website for KEYFO Restaurant & Cafe in Schiedam.

## Positioning
Dutch:
KEYFO Restaurant & Cafe
Turkse grill en döner in Schiedam.

Turkish:
KEYFO Restaurant & Cafe
Schiedam’da Türk grill ve döner.

## Primary Goals
- Show the menu quickly.
- Drive WhatsApp and phone pickup orders.
- Make route/location easy.
- Show delivery via Thuisbezorgd as a secondary option.
- Build trust with real photos, rating, opening hours and clear NAP data.

## Scope
Included:
- Static restaurant website
- Dutch route /
- Turkish route /tr/
- HTML menu
- WhatsApp / phone / route CTAs
- Thuisbezorgd secondary CTA
- SEO/AEO files and structured data

Excluded:
- CMS
- Backend
- Database
- Online payment
- Reservation system
- Checkout/cart
- Chatbot
- PDF menu
- Review widget

## Tone
Warm, local, appetizing, professional, direct.
Not fine dining. Not fake luxury.

## Important Clarification
KEYFO does not have online ordering or payment on its own website.
Pickup orders are handled through phone or WhatsApp.
Delivery is only offered externally through Thuisbezorgd.
The website links to Thuisbezorgd as a secondary external CTA.
Do not build a cart, checkout, payment flow, account system or internal ordering system.
